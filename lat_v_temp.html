Temperature



An inverse relationship appears between the latitude and temperature. However, some of the highest temperatures are at the -20 and 20-degree latitude. Temperature is complex and includes many variables; thus, local factors like altitude, regional climate, topography, or even the Earth's seasonal tilt could cause those highs.
